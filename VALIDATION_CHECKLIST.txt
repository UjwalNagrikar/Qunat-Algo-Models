═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 7: PARAMETER TUNING (If Needed)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If results are NOT acceptable:

Problem: Too many losses (Win Rate < 35%)
  → Reduce aggressive settings:
    • Lower KELLY_CAP from 3.5 to 2.5
    • Raise long threshold (fewer trades)
    • Raise short threshold (fewer shorts)

Problem: Too few trades (< 15 in test)
  → Increase signal frequency:
    • Lower signal thresholds
    • Reduce MIN_HOLD from 2 to 1
    • Increase exposure targets

Problem: High drawdown (> 40% in test)
  → Tighten risk controls:
    • Reduce TRAIL_STOP from 3.5% to 2.5%
    • Lower MAX_HOLD from 15 to 10
    • Cap position sizing

Problem: Overfitting (Train >> Test performance)
  → Reduce complexity:
    • Remove shortest-term features
    • Increase MIN_HOLD (hold positions longer)
    • Use simpler signal logic
